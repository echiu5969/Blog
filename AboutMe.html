ABOUT ME

My ideal first date would be doing something wild and funny, then grab some dinner and in the end, go for a long walk and enjoy some of the scenery. My hobbies are playing the guitar, dancing, and photographing dope things! But most people know me as the dancer! This blog contains a bunch of mostly random things but will also include a lot of my best and worst moments in my life. The site is still not quite perfect yet.
