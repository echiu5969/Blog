How Bboying Saved Me!

12-13-2016

I wasn't always this outgoing and crazy guy. I used to be pretty reserved and quiet kid. I didn't really have a hobby. But one day I was on the internet and I saw these crazy videos of people bboying. Since that day, I started honing my skill as a bboy. I started bboying around the age of 16 and since then I usually practice 2 times a week for a couple of hours. I am currently in the breakdance club in the University at Buffalo. Kids, come through to the club, best thing you can do for yourself. For those who are very hesitant on joining a club, let me just tell you that it is a great way to make friends and after a while, it becomes like a second family.

NEVER LIE TO YOUR PARENTS!

12-13-2016

It was a friday and the school day just ended. So I did what most kids did after a friday. I went out to hang out with some friends. After a couple of hours, me and my boys landed in Wendy's. My mom started calling me asking if I was coming home. I kept telling her I was waiting for the train but I was actually talking with my friend at Wendy's. Ironically, my mom's name is Wendy also. When I got home, I literally got my butt whooped. Not the best experience but I learned never to lie again.

College Life Sucks!

12-13-2016

The first semester of college is coming to an end and all I have to say about my experience about college is that it sucks! Literally had some of my most embarassing and stressful days in college. It's cool that I have more freedom but know I regret wanting to grow up. College exams got me going crazy and every grades matter. Being far from home is not helping too. The food is not that bad but compared to food at NYC, the food is nothing. Winter break is coming up soon and I have one more final before I can safely say that I finished my first semester of college.
